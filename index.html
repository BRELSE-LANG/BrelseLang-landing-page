What is BrelseLang?

BrelseLang is a revolutionary hybrid language that combines Braille and Morse Code, designed for cybersecurity, assistive technology, and tactile computing. It empowers communication through touch and vibration.

Use Cases

  • Accessible communication for visually impaired users
  • Secure transmission through tactile-only channels
  • Offline encoding/decoding for remote or critical environments
